About PJ Sushi

The most frequently asked question is, what does PJ stand for? These initials stand for Patrick and Jasmine, the names of the owners of this family-run sushi restaurant in Fort Lee, New Jersey, opened in February of 2010.

We serve delicious, filling, and great quality sushi for affordable prices.

On Google Reviews, we have a rating of 4.7 stars and on Yelp, we have an overall rating of 4 stars.
